Relating my Values to Stereotype Threat

I am a young white male, and I don't feel like I am affected by stereotype threat very often. As an outsider looking in, I can sympathize with people undergoing stereotype threat. I feel all the values I have listed would be jarred by stereotype threat. By re-affirming one's values and believing they embody them a person could overcome stereotype threat. I feel like breathing exercises help me when I am stressed.
